About Me

I'm Robin, an engineering student at Cornell University from Toronto. I am passionate about emerging technologies and creating positive social impacts in society through innovation.


Currently, I'm interested in machine learning, robotics, quantum computing, and photonics. I am the founder and CTO of Biofy, a startup company working to improve food security across Canada through the implementation of miniature anaerobic digesters. Previously, I worked as a research intern at the Helmy Group at the University of Toronto Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, as well as the Department of Physics at the National University of Singapore.
